Welcome to Book Reviews!

Our mission is to help our users to find the books they would like to read and to share their opinion about books with other readers.

Some things you can do on our website:

You can search for a book by ISBN, title, author and year of publication. You will see the list of all the books (with information about ISBN, title, author, year), we are able to find on your request.

On the page of each book you can find additional information such as a description, our rating, ratings from a broader audience (Goodreads.com and Google Books) and reviews left by other users on our website.

You can write your own review and rate the book on a scale of 1 to 5.

To be able to see book details, reviews made by other readers or to leave your own review you have to register for our website and log in.

We also have an API endpoint, which allows you to get book information in the JSON format.

API information:

Endpoint: /api/{ISBN}

Method: GET

Success response example:


    {
        "title": "Memory",
        "author": "Doug Lloyd",
        "year": 2015,
        "isbn": "1632168146",
        "review_count": 28,
        "average_score": 5.0
    }

Error response example (with status code 404 Not found):


    {
        'error': 'Invalid book ISBN'
    }
